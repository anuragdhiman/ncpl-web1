About Us

Neptune Controls was established in UAE about 8 years back and is present in Ajman, Abu Dhabi and Qatar. Neptune was invited by different OEM's to start an office in India. Towards this, Neptune Controls was setup in India in January 2011. Today Neptune is headquartered in Bangalore with a branch office in Mangalore.We are a business house with sound financial foundation, dynamic business policies, a great sense of professional ethics, and a team of committed

employees. Our Products & solutions bear testimony of Neptune's long lasting relationship with our global partners in progress. Neptune Controls is constantly evolving, bringing the latest technology and products to its customers and innovating new methods to gain highest customer satisfaction.
